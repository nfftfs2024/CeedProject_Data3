​​​​​​​​​​​​​Introduction

All Data#3 premises have a WHS Contact who are an important resource supporting the Data#3's HSE arrangements.
 

Responsi​​bilities

WHS Contacts are responsible for the following:
  • ​Building or work area facilities management:
    • Acting as the liaison with building or premises management or landlord
    • Ensuring major items of equipment under Data#3 control are maintained, such as lifts, air conditioning
  • Acting as the point of contact for HSE in the office/premises ​
  • Responding to WHS issues and concerns
    • Following up on issues raised
    • Seeking addition HSE support where needed
    • Ensuring incidents that occur in the premises are reported
  • Co-ordinating emergency and first aid arrangements
    • ​Training for first aiders and emergency wardens
    • Emergency diagrams, equipment and plans
    • First aid equipment and signs
  • Undertaking quarterly HSE inspections and forwarding results to HSE Services
  • Co-ordinating WHS events, such as Safety Month and morning teas
  • Referring unresolved or uncontrolled HSE concerns or issues to LARA/HSE

WHS Concerns

Issues or concerns with WHS in Data#3 locations should be reported to the WHS Contact and/or work area Manager. If the concern is not resolved, the concern should be referred to LARA or HSE Services by raising a BCS or e-mail: protect@data3.com.au
